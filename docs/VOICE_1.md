# Ziggy Voice Guide

## Who Is Ziggy
Lead writer of the Daily Weed Newspaper. Been smoking since 14, 
calling out mid since 15. Mascot. The voice. The brand.

Gen-Z energy but not cringe. Savage but accurate.
Never mean to people — only to bad prices and lazy dispensaries.
Celebrates real deals with genuine enthusiasm.
Roasts corporate pricing with zero mercy.

## Core Voice Rules
1. Short sentences. Never more than 2-3 per commentary item.
2. No exclamation points — confidence doesn't need them
3. No "amazing" "incredible" "awesome" — those are marketing words
4. Slang: "mid", "slaps", "hard pass", "finessed", "king", "fr"
5. References: slot machines, tourists, loyalty cards, corporate robbery
6. Never performatively woke, never offensive to people
7. The market is a crime scene. Ziggy is the detective.

## Ziggy's Daily Rating System
- 9-10: "the dispensaries are doing something right today. write it down."
- 7-8: "solid deals, a couple real winners, still room to do better"
- 5-6: "mid day. the market is surviving, not thriving."
- 3-4: "disappointing. the prices are disrespectful."
- 1-2: "corporate robbery in broad daylight. stay home."

## What Ziggy Never Says
- "Amazing deal!" (too marketing)
- "Don't miss out!" (FOMO manipulation)
- "Limited time!" (same)
- "We're excited to..." (corporate)
- "Consumers" (they're people)
- "Utilize" (say "use")
- Anything longer than 3 sentences per item

## Big Mike's Voice (for reference)
Chill. Informed. Local. Never alarmist.
"Word is..." / "Heard from a budtender that..." / "Something's coming."
Gossip energy — he knows things, shares them calmly.

## Tourist Terry's Voice (for reference)  
Practical. Slightly nervous. Grateful.
Talks about Uber costs, walking distance, saving money.
"I took an Uber from the Bellagio. $8 each way. Saved $30 on the eighth. Math is math."
Never condescending to tourists — he IS the tourist.

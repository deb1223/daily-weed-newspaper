# Ziggy’s Drip – Loyalty & Rewards System

**Purpose**  
Turn every Pro subscriber into a motivated evangelist by rewarding daily use, sharing, and loyalty. The system is deliberately generous because loyalty-driven growth is ~10x cheaper than X ads while producing higher-LTV users.

**Core Philosophy**  
- Points are the only currency. You can spend them on physical merch OR digital glove skins — your choice.  
- Milestone gifts are 100% free bonuses on top of your points.  
- We spend zero dollars on ads. Instead we funnel that money back to our users.

**Points Earning Rules**

| Action                              | Points | Frequency / Notes |
|-------------------------------------|--------|-------------------|
| First Pro subscription              | 500    | One-time welcome bonus |
| Every month subscribed              | 200    | Auto on renewal |
| Daily visit (site or newspaper)     | 10     | Max 1 per day |
| Referral visit (unique link click)  | 50     | Every visitor (not just subs) |
| Share edition on X or IG            | 50     | Max 1 per day |
| High-usage bonus                    | 50     | 5+ days used in a month |

**Ziggy’s Drip Shop**  
Users can spend points on:
- Anime glove skins

**Fine Print**  
We reserve the right to determine whether a share is organic and genuine (vs bot-generated or inauthentic). The free milestone gifts are not expected to attract scammers, but we will monitor for fairness.

We figured the Google fat cats and their buddies already have enough ad money coming in, so we decided to funnel our would-be ad spend back to you instead. No ads, just Drip.

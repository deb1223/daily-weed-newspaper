# Daily Weed Newspaper — Marketing Plan

## Core Philosophy
Zero ad spend. Every dollar that would go to Google or Meta goes back to users 
instead via Ziggy's Drip loyalty system. Growth is organic, referral-driven, 
and community-powered.

"We figured the Google fat cats and their buddies already have enough ad money 
coming in, so we decided to funnel our would-be ad spend back to you instead."

---

## Top of Funnel — Free Daily X Posts

**What:** Every morning, post the top 3 deals of the day on X.
**Format:**
```
🌿 Vegas weed deals right now:

1. [Product] @ [Dispensary] — $[price] (was $[original]) [-X%]
2. [Product] @ [Dispensary] — $[price] (was $[original]) [-X%]  
3. [Product] @ [Dispensary] — $[price] (was $[original]) [-X%]

Full report + price comparison dashboard → dailyweednewspaper.com

— Ziggy
```
**Voice:** Ziggy's. Never corporate. Never "check out our deals."
**Automation:** Vercel Cron at 8am pulls top 3 from Supabase, 
posts via X API. Human (Dan) reviews before it goes out.
**Goal:** Build audience, drive traffic, demonstrate value daily.

---

## Middle Funnel — The Free Table Hook

The /prices table is free and genuinely useful.
Users who find it via X will use it, bookmark it, come back.
The Compare Prices modal with the blur + upsell is the conversion moment.
"Pro members see all X dispensaries carrying this — $9/month"

---

## Conversion — Ziggy's Drip

See ZIGGYS-DRIP-LOYALTY-SYSTEM.md for full spec.

Key conversion hooks:
- 500 point welcome bonus makes signing up feel immediately rewarding
- Free rolling tray at 3 months is a physical, visible, shareable reward
- Glove skins tie digital engagement to identity/personality
- Referral points (50 per visitor) make sharing feel worth it

---

## Retention — Daily Email at 8am

The daily Ziggy report keeps Pro subscribers engaged every single morning.
Churn prevention: if you miss it, you feel it.
The email IS the product. Make it good enough that people 
look forward to it like a morning newsletter.

## Guerrilla Marketing

**QR Code Water Bottle Labels (from VegasPlanPro playbook):**
- Custom labels with Ziggy branding + QR code
- Distribute on the Strip, dispensary parking lots, tourist areas
- "Scan for today's best deals in Vegas" — immediate value prop
- Cost: near zero, reach: tourists at point of purchase decision

**Dispensary Staff Relationships:**
- Budtenders recommend products — they also recommend tools
- Give dispensary staff free Pro accounts
- They become advocates: "there's this site that shows you the best deals..."
- Cost: free Pro accounts (~$0 marginal cost)

**Reddit / Local Communities:**
- r/vegaslocals, r/LasVegas, r/weed — post real useful content
- Not spam — actual deal alerts, Ziggy voice, genuine value
- Link in bio, not in post

## Ziggy's Drip as Marketing Engine

The loyalty system IS the marketing system:
- Referral visits earn points → users share links
- Sharing on X/IG earns points → organic social spread
- "Ziggy Legend" tier gets name in the newspaper → 
  aspirational status, people talk about it
- Free merch at milestones → walking billboards
- Glove skins → screenshots, social flexing

## City Launch Playbook (repeat per city)

1. Stand up scraper for new city (1-2 days)
2. Announce on X: "Ziggy just landed in [City]. 
   [X] dispensaries. [X] products. Today's best deal: [deal]."
3. Post in local cannabis subreddits
4. Find 3-5 local cannabis influencers, give free Pro accounts
5. QR label run in that city if Dan is traveling there
6. Let Ziggy's Drip do the rest — existing subscribers in that 
   area activate and start sharing

## What We Do NOT Do
- No Google Ads
- No Meta Ads  
- No influencer paid deals
- No sponsored content in the newspaper
- No affiliate links (Ziggy's credibility is the product)
- No "limited time offer" manipulation tactics
- No email blasts to bought lists

The credibility IS the product. The moment Ziggy is sponsored, 
he's just another corporate shill. He never becomes that.

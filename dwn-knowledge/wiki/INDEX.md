# DWN Knowledge Base Index

## Terpenes

- [Myrcene](terpenes/myrcene.md) — Myrcene is the couch-lock terpene — the most abundant in cannabis and the engine behind sedative "indica" effects.
- [Limonene](terpenes/limonene.md) — Limonene is the daytime-anxiety terpene — citrus-forward mood lift powered by serotonin, not sedation.
- [Caryophyllene](terpenes/caryophyllene.md) — β-Caryophyllene is the pain-and-inflammation terpene — the only cannabis terpene that directly activates CB2 receptors.
- [Linalool](terpenes/linalool.md) — Linalool is lavender's terpene — GABA-mediated calm that pairs with myrcene to anchor the sleep profile.
- [Pinene](terpenes/pinene.md) — Pinene is the focus-and-memory terpene — the only cannabis compound that directly counteracts THC-induced memory impairment.
- [Terpinolene](terpenes/terpinolene.md) — Terpinolene is the Haze terpene — fresh-piney aroma with sedative preclinical pharmacology that contradicts its sativa marketing.
- [Ocimene](terpenes/ocimene.md) — Ocimene is the sweet-herbal freshness terpene — highly volatile, antifungal, and the light-heady note behind the social-creativity stack.
- [Humulene](terpenes/humulene.md) — α-Humulene is the earthy pain-relief sesquiterpene — cannabimimetic, anti-inflammatory, and caryophyllene's natural co-pilot.
- [Nerolidol](terpenes/nerolidol.md) — Nerolidol is the sedative sesquiterpene — skin-penetrant, antimalarial, and the sleep-stack's woody-floral depth note.
- [Bisabolol](terpenes/bisabolol.md) — α-Bisabolol is chamomile's sesquiterpene — anti-inflammatory, skin-soothing, and the quietest primary cannabis terpene.

## Combination Profiles

- [Sleep Profile](sleep-profile.md) — Sleep profile is myrcene plus linalool — opioid-pathway body sedation stacked on GABA-mediated mind quieting.
- [Daytime Anxiety Profile](daytime-anxiety-profile.md) — Daytime-anxiety profile is limonene plus pinene — citrus uplift stacked with pine clarity, no sedation.
- [Pain Relief Profile](pain-relief-profile.md) — Pain-relief profile is caryophyllene plus myrcene plus humulene — CB2 activation backed by opioid-pathway depth.
- [Focus Profile](focus-profile.md) — Focus profile is pinene plus limonene — acetylcholinesterase-driven memory clarity stacked with serotonergic alertness, no sedation.
- [Social Creativity Profile](social-creativity-profile.md) — Social-creativity profile is terpinolene plus limonene plus ocimene — the Haze stack for creative euphoria.
- [Body Relief Daytime Profile](body-relief-daytime-profile.md) — Body-relief-daytime profile is caryophyllene plus humulene plus pinene — CB2 anti-inflammatory with daytime function intact.
